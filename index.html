📜 Society Constitution
1

Monthly Contribution

Each family pays R200 on or before the 25th of every month. Late payments incur a R50 penalty.

2

Payout Amount

Upon death of a registered member or their spouse, the society pays out R20,000 within 5 business days of claim approval.

3

Waiting Period

New members must contribute for 3 consecutive months before being eligible for a payout claim.

4

Claim Process

A death certificate must be submitted within 14 days of passing. The committee approves within 48 hours.

5

Missed Payments

Members who miss 2 consecutive months forfeit their payout eligibility until payments are reinstated for 3 months.

6

Committee

The society is governed by a 5-member committee elected annually. Decisions require a 3/5 majority vote.
