Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. PAYMENT PROCESSING

All payments through UMali are processed immediately and securely through our payment partner (Ozow).

Once a payment is successfully processed and transferred to the funeral home, it is FINAL and cannot be reversed.

2. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Refunds are NOT available for:

  • Successfully processed member contributions (these go to funeral home)
  • Installation fees (R499 for funeral homes)
  • Commission fees (6-9% on monthly income)
  • Completed transactions

Refunds MAY be available for:

  • Duplicate payments (if member accidentally pays twice) - within 7 days
  • Technical errors causing incorrect amounts - within 24 hours
  • Payment made to wrong funeral home account - within 24 hours

3. DUPLICATE PAYMENT REFUND PROCESS

If you believe a duplicate payment was made:

Step 1: Contact your funeral home admin immediately

Step 2: Provide transaction reference and date

Step 3: Funeral home submits refund request to UMali within 7 days

Step 4: UMali verifies and processes refund (5-7 business days)

Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

4. TECHNICAL ERROR REFUNDS

If a technical error caused an incorrect payment amount:

Step 1: Report within 24 hours of transaction

Step 2: Provide proof of error (screenshot, transaction details)

Step 3: UMali investigates and verifies

Step 4: Refund processed within 48 hours if error confirmed

5. FUNERAL HOME CANCELLATION

Funeral homes can cancel their UMali account by:

Step 1: Provide 30-day written notice

Step 2: Settle any outstanding fees

Step 3: Account is deactivated after 30 days

Step 4: No refunds for installation fee or past commissions

6. MEMBER PAYMENT DISPUTES

Member payment disputes must be resolved between:

  • The member
  • Their burial society admin
  • The funeral home

UMali does NOT refund member payments as we are a processor only.

7. FAILED TRANSACTIONS

If a payment FAILS during processing:

  • No charges are applied to member
  • No commission is charged to funeral home
  • Member can retry payment
  • UMali investigates if required

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

For payment disputes:

Step 1: Contact your funeral home or burial society admin

Step 2: Provide transaction reference and details

Step 3: Funeral home escalates to UMali if needed

Step 4: UMali investigates (3-5 business days)

Step 5: Resolution or explanation provided

9. PAYMENT PARTNER ISSUES

If Ozow (our payment partner) experiences issues:

  • UMali is NOT responsible for delays or failures
  • Contact Ozow directly for payment-related issues
  • UMali assists with escalation if needed

10. CHARGEBACK REQUESTS

Chargebacks through your bank will:

  • Delay resolution
  • Incur additional fees
  • Potentially suspend your account

Please contact UMali directly first for faster resolution.

11. REFUND TIMELINE

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • EFT refunds: 5-7 business days
  • Card refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Mobile wallet: 1-2 business days

Refunds go to the ORIGINAL payment method used.

12. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

UMali may approve refunds in special circumstances:

  • Death of member (contribution returned to next-of-kin)
  • Genuine hardship cases (reviewed individually)
  • System errors caused by UMali (100% refund)

Each case is reviewed individually.
