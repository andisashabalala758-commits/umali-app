About UMali
🇿🇦 Made in South Africa
Built for our communities. Built for our people.
UMali is Africa's first complete digital platform that manages burial societies, tracks stokvel contributions, and connects grieving families to funeral services — all in one app.
👤
Andisa Shabalala
Founder & CEO · UMali
I grew up watching burial society admins struggle with notebooks, WhatsApp arguments about who paid, and families waiting days for funeral payments during the most painful moments of their lives. I knew there had to be a better way. At 17 years old, I built UMali from nothing — no money, no investors, just a phone and a vision. I wanted to build something that serves real South African communities, not just people who can afford fintech apps. UMali is for Mama Dlamini running a 30-family burial society in Soweto. It is for the stokvel group in Khayelitsha saving for December. It is for every South African family who deserves dignity when they bury their loved ones.

What UMali Does
UMali is the first app in Africa that combines all of these in one platform:
💰
Stokvel Management
Create and manage any type of stokvel — savings, grocery, burial. Track contributions, manage payouts and send automatic reminders.
⚰️
Burial Society Management
Full admin dashboard for burial society leaders. Member management, contribution tracking, claim filing and approval — all digital.
🕊️
Funeral Services Marketplace
Browse, compare and book trusted funeral companies across South Africa. Burial society payout sent directly to funeral home within minutes.
⚡
Instant Funeral Payments
Once a claim is approved, UMali automatically pays the funeral home and notifies all society members. No waiting. No manual transfers.
🏦
All SA Banks Connected
FNB, Capitec, Standard Bank, ABSA, Nedbank, TymeBank, Discovery Bank and African Bank — all connected through one secure integration.

POPIA Compliant
This policy complies with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) No. 4 of 2013. Your data rights are protected by South African law.
Welcome to UMali. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how UMali (Pty) Ltd collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information when you use our platform.
By using UMali, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.
📌 Simple Summary
We collect only what we need to run your burial society or stokvel. We never sell your data. We never share it without your permission except to process payments. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

2
What Information We Collect
We collect the following personal information when you register and use UMali:
👤 Personal Identity Information
• Full name
• South African ID number (for KYC verification)
• Date of birth
• Profile photo (optional)
📞 Contact Information
• Phone number (required for OTP verification)
• Email address
• Physical address (for burial society membership)
💰 Financial Information
• Bank account details (encrypted and stored securely)
• Contribution payment history
• Stokvel and burial society balances
• Transaction references
⚰️ Burial Society Information
• Society membership details
• Contribution records
• Claim information (including deceased details)
• Death certificates (uploaded by members)
We do NOT collect:
  • Your banking passwords or PINs
  • Your exact location (only province for funeral search)
  • Social media information
  • Information about your children without consent
3
Why We Collect Your Information
We collect your information only for these specific purposes:
  • To create and manage your account on UMali
  • To manage your burial society or stokvel membership and contributions
  • To process payments for contributions and funeral bookings
  • To verify your identity as required by South African financial regulations
  • To send you notifications about payments, claims and society updates
  • To connect you to funeral services when a burial claim is approved
  • To generate financial statements for your society or stokvel
  • To detect and prevent fraud on our platform
  • To comply with South African law including POPIA and FICA
4
Who We Share Your Information With
We share your information only with the following parties and only when necessary:
✅ Payment Processors
PayFast and Ozow receive your payment information to process contributions and funeral payments. They have their own POPIA-compliant privacy policies.
✅ Funeral Companies (Only When You Book)
When a burial claim is approved and a funeral booking is made, we share your contact details and burial requirements with the selected funeral company only.
✅ Bank Integration (Stitch Money)
To verify bank payments, we use Stitch Money's read-only banking API. We never share your banking password. Only transaction verification data is accessed.
❌ We NEVER Share With
• Advertisers or marketing companies
• Data brokers
• Other burial societies or stokvels without your consent
• Any third party for commercial purposes
• Government unless required by law
5
How We Protect Your Information
We use industry-leading security measures to protect your data:
  • AES-256-GCM encryption — the same standard used by major South African banks
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) — OTP required for every login
  • Biometric authentication — Face ID and fingerprint support
  • AI fraud detection — monitors every transaction for suspicious activity
  • Firebase Security Rules — your data is only accessible by you
  • Encrypted database storage — on Google's secure servers
  • Secure HTTPS connection — all data in transit is encrypted
  • Regular security audits — to identify and fix vulnerabilities
⚠️ Important
No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at umaliapp@gmail.com and use the Account Freeze feature in the app.
6
How Long We Keep Your Information
We keep your personal information for the following periods:
  • Active account data: For as long as your account is active
  • Financial transaction records: 5 years (required by South African law)
  • Burial claim records: 7 years (for legal and insurance purposes)
  • Security logs: 2 years
  • Deleted account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of your request
7
Your Rights Under POPIA
As a South African citizen, POPIA gives you the following rights:
👁️
Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you at any time.
✏️
Right to Correction
You can ask us to correct any inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
🗑️
Right to Deletion
You can request that we delete your personal information. We will do so within 30 days except where we are required by law to keep it.
🚫
Right to Object
You can object to us processing your personal information for certain purposes including direct marketing.
📦
Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a portable format to transfer to another service.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at umaliapp@gmail.com with the subject line "POPIA Data Request". We will respond within 30 days.
8
Cookies and Tracking
UMali uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:
  • Session cookies: To keep you logged in securely
  • Security cookies: To detect fraud and protect your account
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve the app (no personal identification)
We do NOT use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not display advertisements in UMali.
9
Children's Privacy
UMali is designed for adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18 without verifiable parental consent.
If you believe we have collected information from a minor without consent, please contact us immediately at umaliapp@gmail.com.
