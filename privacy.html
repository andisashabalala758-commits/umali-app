Privacy & POPIA Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

UMali respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), 2013.

2. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

For Funeral Homes:

  • Business name and registration number
  • Owner/director name and ID number
  • Business address
  • Contact details (email, phone)
  • Bank account details
  • Transaction history

For Members:

  • Name
  • Identification number
  • Contact information
  • Payment history
  • Beneficiary information

For Individual Policy Holders:

  • Name and ID number
  • Contact information
  • Age (calculated from ID)
  • Beneficiary details
  • Payment history

Technical Information:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser type
  • Access logs
  • Usage patterns

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Process payments securely
  • Verify your identity and business legitimacy
  • Maintain accurate records
  • Generate reports and statements
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and security breaches
  • Improve our platform functionality
  • Communicate important updates

4. DATA SHARING

Your information is NOT shared with third parties except:

  • Payment processor (Ozow) - for transaction processing
  • Regulatory authorities - when legally required
  • Your funeral home or burial society - relevant information only
  • Law enforcement - when legally required

5. DATA SECURITY

We protect your information using:

  • Firebase encryption (industry-standard)
  • Secure authentication (email + password)
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Limited staff access (need-to-know basis)
  • Regular security reviews

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for:

  • Active accounts: For the duration of your membership
  • Inactive accounts: 3 years for regulatory compliance
  • Payment records: 7 years (tax compliance)

You can request deletion of non-essential data at any time.

7. YOUR RIGHTS (POPIA)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of non-essential data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing
  • Lodge complaints with the Information Regulator

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

UMali is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

If we discover a child is using the platform, we will delete their account and notify their guardian.

9. COOKIES AND TRACKING

UMali uses minimal cookies for:

  • Session management (login persistence)
  • User preferences
  • Security purposes

You can disable cookies in your browser settings (may affect functionality).
